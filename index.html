How to Play

Objective: Reach 225 points .

  1. Anchor rows: Lines 1 and 7 are fixed anchor words (not scored). Build legal 5-letter words on lines 2–6. (Lines 3-6 will appear as you progress.)
  2. Isograms only: 5-letter words with no repeating letters.
  3. No column duplication: The same letter cannot repeat in a column across rows.
  4. Row scoring: Sum of letter values × row multiplier (rows 2–6 = 1×,2×,3×,5×,10×).
  5. Bonus (+10): Successfully use 5 of the 10 red anchor letters to create your first word on line 2.
  6. Bonus (+25): Reveal all unique letters present in the two anchor words.
  7. Final score: Unused letters’ values are subtracted from your total.
  8. Mastery Goal: After penalties, if your final score is ≥ 225, you win!
